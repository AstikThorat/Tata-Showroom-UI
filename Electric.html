Tata Nexon

The last model on our list of best Tata electric cars in 2024 is the Nexon EV. Like other models in its carmaker’s portfolio, the Nexon EV is available in two variants. Dubbed Prime and Max, these offer different battery packs and performance. Of course, they’re even priced considerably differently from each other. The Prime model gets a 30.2 kWh lithium ion battery pack while the Max version features a bigger 40.5 kWh unit. Tata Tiago

The 2-door MG Comet EV notwithstanding, the Tiago EV is currently the most affordable electric car in the mainstream car market of the country. This Tata electric car is based on the conventional, ICE-equipped model but has a totally different character owing to its electric powertrain. This electric car is available in two variants, which means one can purchase a Tiago EV based on their exact requirements and budget. The two battery pack options include a 19.2 kWh unit and a 24 kWh unit, while the peak power is rated at 60 bhp and 74 bhp, respectively. The claimed driving range on a full charge is up to 310 km. The Tiago EV is quite well priced and makes for an excellent intra-city commuter.

TATA Tigor

Even though the Tigor was derived from the Tiago and was launched a couple of years later, its electric version arrived before that of the hatchback sibling. The Tigor EV is easily among the more successful electric sedans to have ever been available in the market. In fact, its popularity is not limited to private car buyers and it’s quite successful even in the taxi segment. The electric sedan is accordingly available in both commercial and regular variants. The affordable electric sedan is powered by a 26 kWh lithium-ion battery pack that has an IP-67 rating. The electric powertrain outputs a maximum power of 73.75 bhp and a peak torque of 170 Nm. Its driving range on a full charge is rated at 315 km, which makes it an excellent choice for commuting and short highway trips.
